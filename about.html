Welcome to UX Lift

I started this site as a way to organise the collection of useful links I'd been building for years, more for my own benefit than anything. However as it came together, I realised it might be a handy resource for others. I hope you find it useful.

I'm always interested in new content, so if you've made something great or have any links to share, I'd love to hear from you.

Made in Manchester by Matt Kevan

I'm a lead UX designer, currently at ao.com. I've been designing and building digital products for many years, including leading the UX and product teams at Mumsnet, and working for a wide range of national and international clients.

Technical notes

This site is built with Jekyll and Bootstrap, stored in Github and hosted by Netlify. The font is Plex Sans.
